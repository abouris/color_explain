256 Color Palette Explainer

The Classic 256 Color Palette

The 256 color palette, also known as the 8-bit color palette, was a significant milestone in computer graphics. It was widely used in early color computer systems and is still relevant in certain contexts today. This palette consists of:

  • 16 standard system colors
  • 216 web-safe colors
  • 24 grayscale shades
